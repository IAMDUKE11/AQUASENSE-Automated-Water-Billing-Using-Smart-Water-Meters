How It Works

Smart Sensors

IoT Flow Sensors installed at your premises measure every drop in real-time.

Instant Transmission

Data travels instantly via ESP8266 technology to our secure cloud servers.

Intelligent Analysis

Our system detects leaks, calculates usage, and prepares your automated bill.

You Take Control

View graphs, pay via EcoCash, and chat with AI support on this dashboard.

Seamless Payments

Pay your bill instantly using **EcoCash** or **Bank Cards**. Our automated workflow updates your balance the second the transaction clears.

Leak Detection

Don't wait for a high bill. Our system identifies abnormal consumption patterns in real-time and sends **instant alerts** so you can fix leaks early.

AI Smart Assistant

Need help? Ask our AI chatbot: "What was my usage last week?" or "Why is my bill higher?" for instant troubleshooting.

Visual Tracking

View interactive bar graphs tracking your weekly usage. Download detailed **Monthly Reports** to audit your own consumption history.

Conservation & Awareness

We empower you with knowledge. By seeing exactly how much water you use, you can make informed decisions to conserve water, save money, and help the environment.
